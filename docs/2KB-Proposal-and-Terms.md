## Scope of Work

Development and ongoing maintenance of the **2KB Intelligence Platform** — a purpose-built ESPC lifecycle management and Owner's Rep decision support system, including a dedicated on-premise AI agent for data management and intelligent automation.

### What's Included

**Platform Development (Setup)**
- 15-module ESPC lifecycle platform
- Portfolio Command Center with real-time KPIs
- Asset Intelligence with automated condition analysis
- Facility Benchmarking with EUI calculations
- Financial Modeling (DSCR, NPV, payback analysis)
- Governance & Accountability (milestones, risk, change orders, submittals)
- Construction Oversight with scope deviation detection
- Measurement & Verification with drift detection
- Automated Reporting with QA workflow
- Knowledge Management & institutional memory
- Client Visibility Portal (read-only client access)
- Intelligent Assistant (natural language project queries)
- Document Intelligence (AI-powered data extraction)
- Excel/CSV export on every module
- Timeline & Workflow management
- Technical Drawings viewer

**Dedicated AI Agent (On-Premise)**
- Local AI agent deployed on dedicated hardware at 2KB's office
- Manages all project data, document processing, and intelligent automation
- Runs on-site — your data never leaves your network
- Automated monitoring, alerts, and proactive intelligence
- Continuous learning from your ESPC project history

**Ongoing Maintenance**
- Platform hosting and infrastructure
- Bug fixes and performance optimization
- Security updates and monitoring
- Feature enhancements and improvements
- AI agent management, updates, and tuning
- Priority support
- Data backups and disaster recovery

## Pricing

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|

| **Ongoing Maintenance** (monthly) | $2,500/mo |

### Client-Provided Resources

| Item | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| **Dedicated Hardware** (Mac Mini or laptop) | Provided by 2KB |
| **AI API Costs** (model inference, embeddings) | Paid by 2KB |
| **Internet connectivity** for the on-premise agent | Provided by 2KB |

> **Hardware Recommendation:** Apple Mac Mini M2/M4 (16GB+ RAM) or equivalent laptop. This device will host the AI agent locally, keeping all data processing on-premise for maximum security and privacy. Estimated cost: $600–$1,200 one-time.

### Terms

- **Setup fee** due upon agreement execution
- **Monthly maintenance** begins upon platform delivery and acceptance
- **Billing cycle:** Monthly, due net-15
- **Minimum term:** 12 months
- **After initial term:** Month-to-month, cancellable with 30 days written notice
- **AI costs** (API usage for model inference) are the responsibility of 2KB Engineering Consulting and billed directly by the AI provider
